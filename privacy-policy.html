Pitchside.in — Privacy Policy

Football & Cricket News · English & Telugu

Effective date: June 21, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 21, 2026

Pitchside.in ("we", "our", or "us") operates as a sports news and content platform focused on football and cricket, publishing content in English and Telugu via Instagram (@pitchside.in) and Facebook (PitchSide.IN). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Social media profile information — when you interact with our Instagram or Facebook pages (likes, comments, follows, direct messages), we may receive your public profile data as provided by those platforms.
  • Email address — if you contact us directly via email.
  • Usage data — aggregated, non-personal analytics about how our content is viewed and engaged with, as provided by Instagram and Facebook Insights.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, addresses, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Publish and distribute sports content to our followers
  • Respond to messages or enquiries you send us
  • Understand audience engagement to improve our content
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

To operate Pitchside.in, we use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Meta (Instagram & Facebook) — content publishing and audience engagement. Meta Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-generated captions and content drafting. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs — AI voice generation for Telugu voiceovers. ElevenLabs Privacy Policy
  • imgbb — image hosting for match result cards. imgbb Privacy Policy
  • football-data.org — football match data API. football-data.org Privacy Policy
  • Railway — cloud infrastructure hosting our automation services. Railway Privacy Policy
  • Google (Sheets & Drive) — content calendar management. Google Privacy Policy
  • n8n — workflow automation platform. n8n Privacy Policy

4. Cookies and Tracking

Pitchside.in does not operate its own website with cookies or tracking scripts. Our content is distributed through Instagram and Facebook, which may use their own cookies and tracking technologies. Please refer to Meta's cookie policy for details.

5. Data Retention

We retain any personal information (such as email correspondence) only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, after which it is deleted.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to our processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for general sports audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
